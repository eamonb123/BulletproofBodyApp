# Candy Serving Size Research - "Size People Actually Buy"

**Rule:** Use the size you grab at a checkout line or gas station.
- Candy bars = KING SIZE
- Candy bags = SHARING SIZE
- Protein/swap bars = single bar (as sold)

**Research Date:** 2026-03-10

---

### 9. Jolly Rancher Hard Candy
- **Standard bag at checkout:** 7 oz bag
- **Per 3 pieces:** 70 cal (23 cal per piece)
- **Current DB value:** 93 cal for 4 pieces -- SLIGHTLY HIGH (should be ~92 cal for 4 pieces, close enough)
- **The real question:** Nobody eats just 4 pieces from a 7 oz bag. A 7 oz bag has ~49 pieces = ~1,127 cal total. But hard candy is different -- people graze over hours/days.
- **Realistic single-sitting consumption (10-15 pieces):** 230-345 cal
- **RECOMMENDATION:** Keep at ~70 cal per 3 pieces but note this is grazing candy, not a single-sitting item. The 93 cal / 4 pieces is close (actual is ~92 cal). Not a major issue.
- **Source:** [CalorieKing](https://www.calorieking.com/us/en/foods/f/calories-in-candy-hard-candy-average-all-flavors/ayYWhBSXSSybMYtEdxEOfA), [FatSecret](https://foods.fatsecret.com/calories-nutrition/jolly-rancher/hard-candy)
