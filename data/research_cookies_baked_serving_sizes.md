## ORIGINALS

### 1. Oreo Cookies
- **Per cookie:** 53 cal (label serving = 2 cookies / 140 cal)
- **Realistic sitting (6 cookies):** 318 cal
- **Current value:** 320 cal for 6 cookies
- **VERDICT: CORRECT.** 320 cal rounds appropriately. 6 cookies is the right sitting amount (3x label serving).
- **Source:** [FatSecret](https://foods.fatsecret.com/calories-nutrition/oreo/chocolate-sandwich-cookies), [EatThisMuch](https://www.eatthismuch.com/calories/the-original-oreo-1814861)

### 2. Chips Ahoy Original

- **Note:** Label serving is 3 cookies (160 cal). Per cookie = 53 cal.
- **Source:** [CalorieKing](https://www.calorieking.com/us/en/foods/f/calories-in-cookies-chocolate-chip-cookies/MzFujoNzQ2mOktwZ0s264Q), [SmartLabel](https://smartlabel.mondelez.info/044000033385-0001-en-US/index.html)

## KEY FINDINGS

1. **Cookie sitting size should be standardized at 6 cookies** across Oreos, Chips Ahoy, Nutter Butter, and Thin Mints for consistency. Thin Mints are smaller so 8 is reasonable.

2. **Several originals were overestimated:** Cosmic Brownies (340 vs 290), Twinkies (350 vs 280). This makes the swap savings appear worse than reality.

3. **One original was massively underestimated:** Entenmann's donuts (370 vs 580 for 2). This actually makes the swap even more compelling.

4. **Flings was significantly overestimated at 200 cal** -- actual is 140 cal, making it an even better swap than shown.

5. **Prime Bites Birthday Cake was significantly underestimated at 170** -- actual is 230 cal. Still a good swap vs. originals but not as dramatic.

6. **Legendary Foods Chocolate Donut is sold as 2-pack** at 160 cal total, not 190 per donut. This is actually an incredible swap vs. Entenmann's.

7. **HighKey Sugar-Free Sandwich Cookies at 60 cal / 2 cookies IS real** -- confirmed across multiple sources. These use erythritol and are genuinely very low calorie.
