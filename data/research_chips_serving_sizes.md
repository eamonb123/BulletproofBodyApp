## KEY FINDINGS

1. **Most originals were UNDERREPORTED** - Many were using "per serving" calories instead of "per bag" calories. The real gas station grab bag is typically 2.5-3.25 oz, which is 2-3 servings.

2. **Several swaps were slightly off** - Wilde chips were significantly underreported (140 listed vs 170-210 actual). Whisps were overreported because the actual single-serve bag is only 0.63 oz, not 1 oz.

3. **The calorie gap is even LARGER than shown** - When you compare the real grab bag originals (375-490 cal) to the protein swaps (100-170 cal), the savings are massive.

4. **Tostitos Scoops and Carnivore Snax don't have true gas station grab bags** - These aren't really "grab and go" items in the same sense.
