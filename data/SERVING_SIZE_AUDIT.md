# Serving Size Audit — 2026-03-10

## Rule: "Size People Actually Buy"

Every original snack should use the calorie count for the **package you grab at a gas station, checkout line, or vending machine.**
- Chips: single-serve grab bag (typically 2-3 oz)
- Candy bars: king size (that's what's at every register)
- Candy bags: sharing size bag (3-4 oz)
- Ice cream: full pint (people eat the whole thing)
- Drinks: full bottle (20 oz soda, etc.)
- Cookies: realistic sitting (4-6 cookies)
- Cereal: 1 bowl with milk
