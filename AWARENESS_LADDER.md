# Awareness Ladder — Bulletproof Body Funnel

> How client awareness evolves from first touch to close.
> Source: Session 10 (2026-03-09), derived from the live funnel build.
> For use by `/lead-magnet-ask-company` and sales strategy.

---

## The Framework

Eugene Schwartz's 5 levels of awareness, mapped to our funnel:

```
UNAWARE → PROBLEM AWARE → SOLUTION AWARE → PRODUCT AWARE → MOST AWARE
```

Each stage of our funnel moves the prospect exactly ONE level forward. Never skip. Never lecture ahead of where they are.

---

## Stage 1: UNAWARE → PROBLEM AWARE

**Where:** `/bible` hero screen
**Copy:** "Lose your next 10 lbs without giving up any of these."
**Mechanic:** Scrolling marquee of restaurant logos they recognize (Chipotle, CAVA, Panda Express)

**What they believe BEFORE:**
- "I need to eat clean to lose weight"
- "Fast food = bad"
- "I'd have to give up everything I love"
- "Dieting means restriction"

**What shifts:**
- Wait... I can eat at these places AND lose weight?
- The logos are familiar. This isn't some weird meal plan.
- Curiosity fires: "How?"

**The trigger:** "Pick one. I'll prove it." — dares them to test it.

**Mindset after:** "Okay, I'm curious. Show me."

---

## Stage 2: PROBLEM AWARE → SOLUTION AWARE

**Where:** `/bible` restaurant detail view (swap cards)
**Mechanic:** Pick a restaurant → see their usual order → see the swap → see calories saved → see weekly/monthly projection

**What they believe BEFORE:**
- "Maybe there's a trick, but it probably doesn't apply to MY food"
- "This is probably some generic advice"

**What shifts:**
- Holy shit, my Chipotle bowl is 1,130 calories?
- The swap is 470 calories and it's basically the same thing?
- That's 660 calories saved PER VISIT?
- The projection graph shows real fat loss from this ONE swap

**Key emotional moment:** The side-by-side visual. Same restaurant, same vibe, dramatically different numbers. This is the "clean is not clear" moment from the VSL, experienced firsthand.

**Mindset after:** "This actually works. The math checks out. What else can I swap?"

---

## Stage 3: SOLUTION AWARE → PRODUCT AWARE

**Where:** `/bible` gate screen (after 2 swaps) + `/experiments/dark-landing` crossroads
**Copy:** "You've already saved [X] calories from 2 meals. Imagine this for everything you eat."
**Mechanic:** Progress banner tracks cumulative savings. Gate screen appears after 2 swaps with 3 paths.

**What they believe BEFORE:**
- "Cool tool. I can probably figure this out myself."
- "Do I really need help with this?"

**What shifts:**
- They see the cumulative impact — 2 swaps = meaningful deficit
- They realize: every snack, every grocery run, every restaurant... there are hidden calories EVERYWHERE
- The scale of the opportunity becomes clear: "I can't map all of this myself"
- Two CTAs plant the seed:
  - "Want this done for everything you eat?" → there's a SERVICE that does this
  - "See why this works" → there's a deeper explanation

**The fork:**
- Analytical types → VSL (need the science, the proof, the methodology)
- Ready-to-go types → Concierge page (show me the experience)
- DIY types → keep browsing (they come back later)

**Mindset after:** "There's something bigger here. This isn't just a tool."

---

## Stage 4: PRODUCT AWARE → MOST AWARE

**Where:** `/concierge` page OR `/vsl`

### Path A: Concierge Page
**For:** People who want the EXPERIENCE explained
- Problem section: "You know what to eat. You just don't do it."
- Protocol section: Phase 1 (find maintenance) → Phase 2 (swap ecosystem)
- Social proof: metrics, testimonials
- Founder section: Eamon IS the ideal client (engineer, entrepreneur, was out of shape)
- CTA: Book a strategy call

### Path B: VSL
**For:** People who need the SCIENCE explained
- Money analogy (calories = currency, fat bank = savings)
- Myth busting (eat less? no. eat clean? no. it's about WHAT within what you eat)
- RMR equation (math, not motivation)
- Phase 1/2 methodology
- The swap philosophy with real examples
- CTA: Book a strategy call

**What they believe BEFORE:**
- "Interesting tool, but is this legit?"
- "What's the actual program?"
- "Can I trust this person?"

**What shifts:**
- The methodology is sound (not a fad, not restriction)
- The founder has credibility (not a fitness influencer, a guy who solved it for himself)
- Real people got real results (testimonials)
- The promise is specific: "1 lb of fat per week, sustainably, from food you already eat"

**Mindset after:** "I want this. I need to talk to someone."

## Stage 5: MOST AWARE → DECISION

**Where:** Strategy Call (Zoom, booked from concierge or VSL)

**What they believe BEFORE:**
- "I'm interested but I need to know: is this for ME specifically?"
- "What does it actually cost?"
- "Can I trust Eamon with my situation?"

**What happens on the call:**
- Eamon qualifies: Are they coachable? Do they have the problem?
- Present Gold vs Silver based on energy/need level
- Gold ($7k/12wk): "I build everything for you. Concierge. Done."
- Silver ($500/mo x 12): "Same system. Community drives you. You drive yourself."
- Payment plan offered if needed

**The close:**
- "Which one sounds like the best option for you right now?"
- The tool already proved the concept. The VSL/concierge explained the system. The call is about fit and commitment.

**Mindset after:** "I'm in. Let's go."

## The Key Insight

Each stage does ONE job:

| Stage | Job | Emotional Shift |
|-------|-----|-----------------|
| 1. Hero | Create curiosity | "Wait, really?" |
| 2. Swap experience | Prove the method | "Holy shit, the math works" |
| 3. Gate/Crossroads | Reveal the scale | "I can't do all this myself" |
| 4. Concierge/VSL | Build trust in the system | "This is legit and this guy gets it" |
| 5. Strategy call | Commit to the container | "I'm in" |

**We never sell on Stage 1.** We never explain methodology on Stage 2. We never show pricing on Stage 3. Each stage earns the right to the next.

---

## What This Means for Content

Every piece of organic content should map to ONE of these stages:

- **Authority content (40%)** → Stages 1-2 (create problem awareness, show the swaps work)
- **Aligned content (30%)** → Stage 3 (mirror their struggle: "I used to think I had to give up everything too")
- **Authentic content (30%)** → Stage 4 (build trust in Eamon as a person)

The free tool IS the top of funnel. Content drives people TO the tool. The tool does the selling.
